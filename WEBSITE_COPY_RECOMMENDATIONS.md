## 🚀 **NEW MESSAGING FRAMEWORK**

### **Core Concept Shift**
**FROM:** Registry (static, database, listing)
**TO:** Network (dynamic, connections, discovery)

### **Key Themes to Emphasize**
1. **Connection over Collection** - Focus on relationships, not just data
2. **Discovery over Search** - Emphasize finding meaningful connections
3. **Community over Database** - Highlight living, growing ecosystem
4. **Opportunity over Listing** - Focus on business growth possibilities

### **Tone Changes**
**FROM:** Formal, institutional, structured
**TO:** Dynamic, connected, community-focused
